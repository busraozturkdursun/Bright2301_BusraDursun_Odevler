Minimal Touches


While modern furniture is undeniably beautiful in its simplicity, the main topic that defines this style is about functionality. No piece is useless. This attention to functionality also explains why vintage pieces from this period are still in circulation today. Similar to Scandinavian design, Mid-Century Modern style avoids clutter and unnecessary decorations. Character pieces such as large statues or plants are more common, while small trinkets or collectibles are often overlooked.



Neutral Colors


Beiges, browns, black and more. Emphasizing timelessness, the Mid-Century Modern style stands out with its earthy tones and neutral color palette.
